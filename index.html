About

I am a PhD student at the Genomic Machine Learning Lab, University of Queensland, working on the integration of spatial transcriptomics, histopathology images (H&E), and deep learning to study cancer biology.

My research aims to computationally reconstruct gene regulatory networks across cells and tissues by combining single-cell and spatial omics data with large-scale population genomics, contributing to precision medicine and early disease diagnosis.

Research Interests

  • Spatial transcriptomics and spatial omics integration
  • Deep learning for computational pathology
  • Multi-modal representation learning
  • Gene expression prediction from histology images
  • Precision medicine and cancer risk prediction

Current Project

  • Applying deep learning models to predict spatial gene expression from H&E images in breast cancer.
  • Benchmarking and ensemble learning across foundation models for spatial transcriptomics.
